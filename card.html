Parrots

Parrots are birds of the order Psittaciformes. Many parrots can imitate human speech or other sounds.

  • Scientific Name: Psittaciformes
  • Average Length: Variably[8.6 - 95 cm]
  • Average Lifespan: 25 - 30 years
  • Habitat: Tropical and subtropical regions

Parrots are intelligent and social birds that require mental stimulation and physical exercise to thrive in captivity. The greatest diversity of parrots is found in South America and Australasia.
